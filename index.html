I am Aditya Biswas, a BCA student 🎓 with a background in Science 🧪. I have a strong passion for technology and web development 🌐, and I love building real-world projects while continuously sharpening my technical skills.

My toolkit includes C, Python, HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript 💻, along with tools like Canva, GitHub, and Netlify 🔧. I am currently deep-diving into SQL 📊 to expand my backend knowledge.
